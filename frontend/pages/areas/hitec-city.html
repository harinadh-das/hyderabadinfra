About HITEC City

HITEC City (Hyderabad Information Technology and Engineering Consultancy City) is the beating heart of Hyderabad's IT sector. Home to global technology giants like Microsoft, Google, IBM, and Oracle, this planned development offers world-class infrastructure for both commercial and residential purposes. Its strategic location and excellent connectivity make it a prime destination for IT professionals and businesses.
